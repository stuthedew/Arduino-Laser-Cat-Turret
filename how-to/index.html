Laser Turret Cat Toy

Arduino-based laser turret/automatic cat toy using a pan-tilt rig and Pro Trinket from Adafruit. Random laser path, speed and pauses determined by homebrew Markov chain algorithm.

Parts list

Electronics

  • 1 x 5V Pro Trinket
  • 1 x Laser Diode
  • 1 x Linear Potentiometer w/ detents (clicks) (I could have used a rotary switch like this one from Sparkfun, but I liked the smooth feel of the potentiometer)
  • 1 x each Red, Yellow and Green 5mm LED.

KIT-10 Distract-O-Matic maintained by stuthedew
